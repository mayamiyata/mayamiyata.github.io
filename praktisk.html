- Rysensteens regler ved besøg
Rules of conduct in connection with homestay in Rysensteen Gymnasium’s Global Citizenship programme:
The following rules cover the overall framework of good behaviour for students at Rysensteen Gymnasium.
Basically, these rules should be sufficient also when we have global guests.
1 At Rysensteen Gymnasium we show respect for each other and care for the individual and the community.
2 At Rysensteen Gymnasium we show respect for buildings and furniture and everybody protects school materials.
3 At Rysensteen Gymnasium everybody strives to be a good ambassador when representing the school to the world outside.
Remember that young people in Denmark have a high degree of autonomy and freedom compared to the level of
autonomy our guests are used to in their home countries.
1) The visitors are guests in a private home and therefore expected to follow family rules.
2) The exchange is schoolwork and therefore alcohol will not be served.
3) We suggest that the hosting students and their guests return to the residence at 10 o’clock on weekdays.
4) The exchange is arranged in a spirit of friendship. It provides ample opportunities for academic achievement and cultural and social experiences. To make sure that the basis for friendship is fair, we have decided that we pay when our guests are here and they pay when we are guests in their country.
If everyone follows these few rules, the homestay experience will be a success for guests and hosts alike.

Lavet af: Maya Miyata, Rysensteen Gymnasium
